Levels of Care

Our Programs

Residential Mental Health Treatment

Twenty-four-hour care for adults whose symptoms have outpaced an outpatient setting. Daily psychiatric oversight, a structured trauma-informed therapeutic week, and a small, consistent staff who already know the chart.

Call to learn more

Veteran Mental Health Services

A veteran-owned, veteran-led program for active-duty service members and veterans living with PTSD, trauma, and co-occurring mental health conditions. In-network with TRICARE and TriWest.

Call to learn more

Partial Hospitalization Program (PHP)

Six hours of structured clinical programming, five days a week. The right step down from residential, or the right place to start when symptoms are serious but twenty-four-hour care isn't required.

Call to learn more

Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP)

Three hours of programming, five days a week, with morning (8:00–11:00) or afternoon (12:00–3:00) tracks. The right level of care for clients re-engaging with work, school, or family life without losing clinical rhythm.

Call to learn more
