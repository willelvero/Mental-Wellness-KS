## What happens next

Once we have this form back along with your logo, hero photo, and any other assets, we'll:

1. Build out the homepage and one program/condition page as a first draft.
2. Send those back for your review and your lead practitioner's clinical sign-off.
3. Iterate on copy and design until both of you are happy.
4. Build out the remaining program and condition pages.
5. Walk through pre-launch SEO setup (Google Business Profile alignment, sitemap submission, etc.).
6. Launch.
